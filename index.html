Taiwan is renowned for the diverse street food, including bubble tea, a sweet tea-based drink with chewy tapioca pearls, oyster pancake, a savory pancake filled with oysters and vegetables, and stinky tofu, a pungent and flavorful fermented tofu dish.

These dishes are a must-try for anyone visiting Taiwan.
